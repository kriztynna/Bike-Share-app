Historical station data

Browse historical data for any station. View available bikes, available docks, and out of order stuff, separately or combined. Watch how they've changed over different time spans.

Screen for system stress

When the data don't add up, there are bikes and docks out of order. busybici shows you which stations have the largest number of bad bikes, docks, and errors at the moment.

Watch bike share usage in aggregate

busybici adds up the data from each station to show you in the state of the overall bike share system. Track the availability of bikes, docks, and the incidence of issues through time.
