busybici

A new way to stay informed about available CitiBike bikes and docks in your area. Made for and by users of New York City's bike share program.

Plan your journey

Do you use CitiBike regularly? busybici can help you avoid surprises. Tell it what stations you use and when you need to hear about them. When the time comes, it will send you a quick email or text with the quantity of available bikes (or docks) at your station choices. Think of busybici as a minimalist personal assistant: it does one little thing to nudge your day in the right direction.

Availability of bikes and docks in time series

busybici is always keeping tabs on each station's available bikes and docks, so it can show you some interesting trends. Have a look at your nearest station's available bikes over time, for example, and finally figure out when it usually gets restocked. Or just look at pretty charts for their own sake.

look at pretty charts »

Other fun stuff

busybici has been collecting data on New York City's bike share program since June 2013, shortly after the program launched. It has kept track of system-wide stats and station-specific details alike. It keeps tally of things like record-setting days and the aggregate amounts of bikes and docks available across time. Yeah, you could say busybici gets a little restless sometimes.
